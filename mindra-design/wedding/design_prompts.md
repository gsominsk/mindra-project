# Wedding Theme: Hyper-Detailed Design Prompts (wedding.mindra.com)

**Global Design Language**:
-   **Philosophy**: "The Love Story". Emotional, Soft, Bespoke.
-   **Typography**: Headings: `Playfair Display` (Italic Serif). Body: `Lato` (Light Sans).
-   **Colors**: Background: Cream (#FDFBF7). Text: Charcoal (#333333). Accent: Soft Gold (#D4AF37).
-   **Navigation**: Top Centered. Minimalist and elegant.

---

## 1. Home Page (`/`)
**Prompt**:
> **Type**: High-Fidelity Web Design Mockup (1920x1080).
> **Subject**: Wedding Host Homepage.
> **Layout**: Full-Screen Editorial Cover.
> **Background**: A full-screen photo.
> -   **Image**: A candid, soft-focus shot of a bride and groom laughing. The host is in the background (blurred), smiling warmly.
> -   **Overlay**: A warm, golden-hour gradient overlay (opacity 20%).
> **Typography (Center)**:
> -   **Main Title**: "Creating Memories" in large, italic Serif font (`Playfair Display`). Color: White.
> -   **Subtitle**: "Your story, told beautifully." Small Sans-serif (`Lato`), widely spaced.
> **Navigation (Top)**:
> -   **Style**: Transparent.
> -   **Logo**: "Mindra Weddings" in a script font, centered.
> -   **Links**: "Stories", "About", "Contact" spaced evenly around the logo.
> **Decor**:
> -   **Graphics**: Subtle, hand-drawn floral line art (white, thin stroke) in the corners.

## 4. Portfolio Page (`/portfolio`)
**Prompt**:
> **Type**: High-Fidelity Web Design Mockup.
> **Subject**: Real Weddings Gallery.
> **Layout**: Pinterest / Moodboard Style.
> **Background**: Cream (#FDFBF7).
> **Grid**:
> -   **Composition**: Masonry grid. Images are packed but with white borders (10px) around them, like polaroids.
