# Party Theme: Hyper-Detailed Design Prompts (party.mindra.com)

**Global Design Language**:
-   **Philosophy**: "The Nightclub". Dark, immersive, glowing.
-   **Typography**: Headings: `Clash Display` (Variable, Edgy). Body: `Space Mono` (Tech).
-   **Colors**: Background: #050505. Accents: Soft Blue (#80D8FF), Soft Pink (#F48FB1).
-   **Navigation**: Same "Dock" structure as Base, but with a "Cyberpunk" skin.

---

## 1. Home Page (`/`)
**Prompt**:
> **Type**: High-Fidelity Web Design Mockup (1920x1080).
> **Subject**: Party Event Host Homepage.
> **Layout**: Center-Focused "Hero" Composition.
> **Background**: Deep Black (#000000). Subtle animated smoke/fog texture at the bottom.
> **Visual Elements**:
> -   **3D Shapes**: Floating abstract 3D geometric shapes (chrome spheres, twisted toruses) in the background. They reflect Soft Blue and Soft Pink lights.
> -   **Main Image**: The host is cut out (no background), holding a microphone, in a dynamic "hyping the crowd" pose. He has a rim-light effect (Soft Blue on left, Soft Pink on right).
> **Typography**:
> -   **Headline**: Massive, outlined text behind the host: "MAKE IT" (Top) "LOUD" (Bottom). Font: 'Clash Display', Bold, Outline Only (White stroke), 200px.
> -   **Glitch Effect**: The text has a slight chromatic aberration (red/blue shift).
> **UI Elements**:
> -   **CTA Button**: A pill-shaped button floating below the host. Background: Gradient (Soft Blue to Soft Pink). Text: "BOOK THE HYPE". Glow effect (box-shadow).
> -   **Nav Dock**: Bottom center. Dark glass style (tinted black). Soft Blue border at the bottom.

## 2. Contact Page (`/contact`)
**Prompt**:
> **Type**: High-Fidelity Web Design Mockup.
> **Subject**: Party Booking Page.
> **Layout**: Single Card "Ticket" Layout.
> **Background**: Dark, with a blurred photo of a rave crowd (purple/blue tones) at 20% opacity.
> **Main Card**:
> -   **Shape**: A vertical rectangular card centered on screen. Looks like a VIP pass or ticket.
> -   **Style**: Glassmorphism (Frosted Dark Glass). Soft Pink border (1px).
> -   **Content**:
>     -   **Header**: "SECURE THE DATE". Font: 'Clash Display', White.
>     -   **Form**: Inputs are transparent with a white bottom border. When active, the border turns Soft Blue.
>     -   **Fields**: "NAME", "DATE", "VENUE".
>     -   **Button**: "CONFIRM" - Solid Soft Pink button with Black text.
> **Decor**: A barcode graphic at the bottom of the card.

## 3. Video Page (`/video`)
**Prompt**:
> **Type**: High-Fidelity Web Design Mockup.
> **Subject**: Party Video Gallery.
> **Layout**: "Streamer" / VJ Loop Interface.
> **Background**: Black grid pattern on dark grey background.
> **Main Player**:
> -   **Frame**: Large video player with a glowing Soft Blue drop shadow.
> -   **Content**: Freeze frame of a confetti explosion at a party.
> -   **Overlay**: Large "PLAY" icon (solid white triangle) with a glitch effect.
> **Sidebar (Right)**:
> -   **Playlist**: A vertical list of "Next Up" videos.
> -   **Items**: Small thumbnails with timestamps (e.g., "02:45"). Text: "Club Icon 2024", "Private B-Day Bash". Font: 'Space Mono', Soft Green (#A8E6CF).
> **Nav Dock**: Bottom center, consistent with Home.

## 4. Portfolio Page (`/portfolio`)
**Prompt**:
> **Type**: High-Fidelity Web Design Mockup.
> **Subject**: Party Photo Gallery.
> **Layout**: Horizontal Scroll Snap (Instagram Story style).
> **Composition**:
> -   **Container**: The screen is filled with 3 large vertical image panels side-by-side.
> -   **Images**: High-energy party shots (flash photography, motion blur).
> -   **Hover Effect**: The center image is fully lit. The side images are dimmed.
> -   **Cursor**: The mouse cursor is replaced by a custom graphic: a small "crosshair" target in Soft Green.
> **Typography**:
> -   **Overlays**: Large, bold text rotated 90 degrees on the side of each image: "IBIZA", "MOSCOW", "DUBAI". Font: 'Clash Display', Outline, White.
