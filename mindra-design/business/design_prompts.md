# Business Theme: Hyper-Detailed Design Prompts (business.mindra.com)

**Global Design Language**:
-   **Philosophy**: "The Boardroom". Trust, Stability, ROI.
-   **Typography**: Headings: `Helvetica Now Display` (Bold). Body: `Inter` (Regular).
-   **Colors**: Primary: Navy Blue (#0A192F). Secondary: Slate Grey (#64748B). Background: White/Light Grey.
-   **Navigation**: **Shifted to Top**. Corporate clients expect standard top navigation.
